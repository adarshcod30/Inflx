# AutoStream Agent: Example Conversation Logs

User: Hello!

User: What are your pricing plans?
Agent: We offer two main plans:
- **Basic Plan**: $29/month, includes 10 videos/month at 720p resolution.
- **Pro Plan**: $79/month, includes unlimited videos, 4K resolution, AI captions, and 24/7 priority support.
How many videos do you typically produce in a month?
