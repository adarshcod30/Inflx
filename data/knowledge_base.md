# AutoStream Knowledge Base

## Product Overview

AutoStream is a SaaS platform that provides automated video editing tools
for content creators. It helps creators produce social-ready videos faster
with AI-powered editing, captioning, and formatting.

## Pricing Plans

### Basic Plan
- **Price:** $29/month
- **Video Limit:** 10 videos per month
- **Max Resolution:** 720p
- **Support:** Email support (business hours only)
- **Export Formats:** MP4, MOV
- **Storage:** 10 GB cloud storage

### Pro Plan
- **Price:** $79/month
- **Video Limit:** Unlimited videos
- **Max Resolution:** 4K
- **AI Captions:** Included (auto-generated, multi-language)
- **Support:** 24/7 priority support
- **Export Formats:** MP4, MOV, WebM, GIF
- **Storage:** Unlimited cloud storage
- **Extras:** Custom branding, team collaboration (up to 5 seats)

## Company Policies

### Refund Policy
- Full refund available within the first 7 days of subscription.
- No refunds are issued after 7 days from the date of purchase.
- Refund requests must be submitted via the support portal.

### Support Policy
- Basic Plan users receive email support during business hours (Mon-Fri, 9 AM - 6 PM EST).
- Pro Plan users receive 24/7 live chat and priority email support.
- Average response time: Basic (24 hours), Pro (under 2 hours).

### Cancellation Policy
- Users can cancel their subscription at any time from the account settings.
- Access continues until the end of the current billing cycle.
- No cancellation fees apply.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I upgrade from Basic to Pro mid-cycle?
Yes. When you upgrade, the remaining days on your Basic plan are prorated
and applied as credit toward your Pro subscription.

### What platforms does AutoStream support?
AutoStream supports direct publishing to YouTube, Instagram, TikTok,
Twitter/X, and Facebook. You can also download edited videos locally.

### Is there a free trial?
AutoStream offers a 7-day free trial of the Pro plan for new users.
No credit card required to start.

### Do you offer team or enterprise plans?
The Pro plan includes team collaboration for up to 5 seats. For larger
teams or custom enterprise needs, contact sales@autostream.io.
