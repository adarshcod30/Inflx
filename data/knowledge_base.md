# AutoStream Knowledge Base

## Product Pricing & Features

### Basic Plan
- **Price**: $29/month
- **Features**:
  - 10 videos per month
  - 720p resolution
  - Standard editing tools

### Pro Plan
- **Price**: $79/month
- **Features**:
  - Unlimited videos
  - 4K resolution
  - AI captions
  - 24/7 priority support

## Company Policies

### Refund Policy
- No refunds are provided after 7 days of purchase.
- Within the first 7 days, a full refund can be requested if the service was not used extensively.

### Support Policy
- 24/7 support is available exclusively on the **Pro Plan**.
- Basic Plan users have access to email support with a 24-hour response time.
- All users have access to the online documentation and community forums.

### About AutoStream
AutoStream is a SaaS platform providing automated video editing tools for content creators on platforms like YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok. Our AI-driven engine helps you cut, polish, and optimize your content in seconds.
